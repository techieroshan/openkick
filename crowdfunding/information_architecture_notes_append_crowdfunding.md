Title: Information Architecture Addendum — Crowdfunding

New domain objects
* Offering
  * Links to a case or investigation
  * Offering type Reg D or Reg CF or informational preview
  * Terms minimum investment and target raise and cap and instrument type
  * Timeline open date and close date
  * Status draft or open or funded or closed or cancelled
  * Risk disclosures and offering documents
* Investor
  * KYC verification
  * Accreditation verification when required for Reg D
  * Wallet and escrow linkage
  * Portfolio positions and distributions
* Issuer
  * Law firm or SPV sponsor profile
  * Campaign materials and media
  * Required compliance tasks and filings

Search facets for offerings
* Status
* Category
* State
* Min investment
* Instrument

Core flows
* Investor onboarding KYC and accreditation then invest then escrow capture then confirmations
* Issuer onboarding submit case and documents then underwriting then terms then publish offering
* Updates and communications Q and A and updates feed per offering
* Distributions record keeping and investor statements

Compliance elements
* Prominent risk disclosures on all offering pages
* Clear indication of offering type and eligibility
* No promises of returns or settlement outcomes
* Conflict disclosures for any referral or fee arrangements
