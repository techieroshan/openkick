Title: Branding and Identity Addendum — Crowdfunding

Investment messaging
* Prominent risk warnings before any invest flow
* Avoid any implication of guaranteed returns or settlement outcomes
* Use clear badges for offering type Reg D or Reg CF or preview
* Display escrow and payment partner logos only with permission

Disclosure components
* Risk banner component at top of Offering pages
* Risk acknowledgment modal in invest flow
* Compliance approved badge for updates and media

Color usage
* Use warning amber sparingly to highlight risks and disclosures
* Use success green only for status funded or distribution paid not for promotional language

Copy guidelines
* Use plain language to describe risks such as loss of principal illiquidity and regulatory limits
* Provide links to full disclosures and offering documents near primary calls to action
