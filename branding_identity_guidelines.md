Brand essence
* Purpose. Help people quickly know their rights and claim what they are owed
* Position. The trusted assistant for class actions and settlements
* Personality. Clear. Practical. Respectful. Protective

Name and tagline
* Name. Openkick
* Tagline. Check eligibility. Claim with confidence

Logo
* Primary logo. Wordmark Openkick with a subtle check mark inside the O
* Secondary mark. Circle with a check icon
* Clear space. Height of the O around all sides
* Minimum size. Digital 24 pixels height

Colors
* Primary blue. Trust and clarity
  * HEX 1E5AF6
  * RGB 30 90 246
* Success green
  * HEX 0BB26E
  * RGB 11 178 110
* Warning amber
  * HEX F5A623
  * RGB 245 166 35
* Neutral dark
  * HEX 1F2937
  * RGB 31 41 55
* Neutral light
  * HEX F3F4F6
  * RGB 243 244 246
* Background white
  * HEX FFFFFF

Typography
* Headings. Inter or SF Pro Display
* Body. Inter or system UI
* Code and data. JetBrains Mono for dev docs
* Line length. Aim for 65 to 85 characters

Iconography
* Simple line icons
* Style. Two pixel stroke with rounded corners
* Common icons. Check, calendar, shield, document, lock, gavel

Imagery
* Real people. Inclusive and respectful
* Avoid fear based imagery
* Use screenshots to teach process

Voice and tone
* Plain language
* Short sentences
* No legal jargon unless needed. Explain terms
* Respectful and empathetic

Accessibility
* Color contrast AA or better
* Text resizes to 200 percent
* Keyboard friendly
* ARIA labels for forms
* Captions on video

Content rules
* No promises of payouts
* Always link to official settlement sites
* Display disclosures that Openkick is not a law firm or settlement administrator
* Show how we make money in plain language

Compliance cues
* Include consent receipts for any outreach
* Present seller specific consent choices for phone or text
* Provide clear unsubscribe and data deletion options

Layouts
* Desktop grid. Twelve columns with 24 pixel gutter
* Mobile grid. Four columns with 16 pixel gutter
* Primary button. Blue fill with white text
* Secondary button. Outline neutral dark

Components
* Eligibility card. Title, status chip, deadline, category, official link
* Claim stepper. Steps with progress and help text
* Consent modal. Clear text, named recipients, time stamp shown

Email
* From name. Openkick
* Subject style. Action first then case name
* Include unsubscribe and mailing address

Metrics visuals
* Use sparklines and simple bar charts
* Annotate charts with plain language notes

Security
* Lock icon only where encryption is relevant
* Do not imply outcomes or affiliations
